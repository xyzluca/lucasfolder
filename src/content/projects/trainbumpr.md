_A playful, chaotic, OMNY-inspired web toy built with React + TypeScript + Vite. Tap the reader, watch fate decide, unlock modes, bingo tiles, slang, memes, and neighborhood lore._

## Overview

**TrainBumpr** is an interactive NYC subway fare simulator that captures the chaotic energy of riding the MTA. Stand clear of the closing memes as you tap the OMNY reader and discover whether your fare is accepted, declined, or something entirely unexpected.

## Features

- **Dynamic tap outcomes** - Accepted / Declined / Chaos
- **Unlockable persona modes** - Tourist, Artist, Student, Local
- **Subway Bingo progression** - Complete your NYC transit experience
- **Meme gallery & inside jokes marquee** - Cultural references and subway humor
- **NYC slang dictionary** with speech output
- **Daily transit quote & cultural trivia**
- **Neighborhood tales modal** - Explore local NYC lore
- **Street anthropology feed** - Observations from the underground
- **Shareable capture panel** - Share your transit moments

## Tech Stack

The project uses modern web technologies for a smooth, interactive experience:

- **React + TypeScript** for robust component architecture
- **Vite** for lightning-fast development and builds
- **Tailwind-esque utility styling** with custom injected styles
- **html2canvas** for capturing and sharing moments
- **Tone.js** for planned micro-sound effects

This project celebrates NYC subway culture with humor, interactivity, and a love for the city's transit chaos.
